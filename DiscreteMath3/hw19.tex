\section*{Homework 19}

\paragraph{Homework 19.}
Let $X$ be a nonnegative random variable with a finite positive expectation. Then, for any positive real $a > 0$,
\[
    \Pr\!\bigl(X \ge a \mathbb{E}(X)\bigr) \le \frac{1}{a}.
\]

\medskip

\begin{proof}[解答]
    Markovの不等式より，非負の確率変数$X$と任意の$t>0$について
    \[
        \Pr(X \ge t) \le \frac{\mathbb{E}(X)}{t}
    \]
    が成り立つ．ここで$t = a \mathbb{E}(X)$とおくと，$\mathbb{E}(X) > 0$かつ$a > 0$より$t > 0$であり，
    \[
        \Pr\!\bigl(X \ge a \mathbb{E}(X)\bigr)
        = \Pr(X \ge t)
        \le \frac{\mathbb{E}(X)}{a \mathbb{E}(X)}
        = \frac{1}{a}
    \]
    を得る．よって主張が示された．
\end{proof}
